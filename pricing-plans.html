❓ Frequently Asked Questions

What happens to my work if I cancel?

You retain full ownership and can export all your work. We provide 30 days to download your data after cancellation.

How does the educational discount work?

Students with valid .edu email addresses automatically receive 60% off all plans. Teachers and institutions have additional special pricing.

What are AI tokens and how many do I need?

Tokens measure AI usage. Light users (1-2 projects) need ~5K/month, moderate users need ~15K/month, heavy users need 50K+/month.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate the billing accordingly.
